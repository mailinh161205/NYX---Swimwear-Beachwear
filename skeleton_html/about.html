    The Story of NYX

    Where beauty meets the ocean, where individuality shines under the sunset

    Our Mission

    NYX is not just a beachwear brand; it is a symbol of freedom, confidence, and the radiant beauty of women under the sun.

    We believe that every beach trip is a journey of self-discovery, and your outfit is the inspirational companion. Founded with an endless passion for the ocean and captivating silhouettes, NYX offers premium bikini and one-piece swimwear designs that perfectly combine modern trends with absolute comfort.

    What makes NYX different

    Sustainability

    We are committed to using recycled and eco-friendly materials, minimizing our impact on the ocean and the green planet.

    Exclusive Design

    Each design is created to celebrate the unique beauty of women, from bold cuts to delicate patterns.

    Premium Quality

    Sustainable Materials

    We prioritize the use of recycled and eco-friendly materials, minimizing our impact on the green planet. Each product is made from premium Lycra fabric, featuring 4-way stretch, quick-drying capability, and UV protection, safeguarding your skin.

    • Fabric recycled from ocean plastic bottles
    • Quick-drying, moisture-wicking material
    • UV protection up to UPF 50+
    • 4-way stretch for a perfect fit
    • Durable and fade-resistant colors
